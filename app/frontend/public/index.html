About this layer

Sentinel-5P tropospheric NO₂ column anomaly for the selected date — the day's column minus the pixelwise same-month historical median (previous three years). A display layer for visual interpretation only: it shows a vertical atmospheric column, not ground-level air; colours use a per-date display stretch (not comparable across dates) with display-only smoothing over an oversampled 0.01° grid; and nothing shown here feeds the statistics.

What this prototype does not yet assess

This prototype does not yet classify air-quality episodes or assess persistence across days, spatial extent, ground-monitor corroboration, or meteorology. Daily valid-area coverage varies with clouds and retrieval quality — it is shown with every value — and spatial coincidence with a road or facility is geography, not attribution.
